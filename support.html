Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • 1. How do I enable location services for WiFi Ghost?
    To access SSID information, WiFi Ghost requires location services to be enabled. You can do this by going to System Settings > Privacy & Security > Privacy, selecting Location Services, and enabling it for WiFi Ghost.
  • 2. Why can't I see my WiFi network name (SSID)?
    If location services are turned off, WiFi Ghost won't be able to show the SSID. Ensure that location services are enabled in your system's privacy settings.
  • 3. Can I customize the refresh rate for WiFi data?
    Yes, WiFi Ghost allows you to set the refresh frequency in the app's settings. You can choose the interval that works best for your needs.
  • 4. How do I set WiFi Ghost to launch at startup?
    You can enable WiFi Ghost to launch at login by selecting the "Launch at Login" option from the app's menu.
