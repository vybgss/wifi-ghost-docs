Privacy Policy for WiFi Ghost

Last updated: October 2, 2024

At WiFi Ghost ("we", "us", or "our"), your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how our macOS application WiFi Ghost operates, emphasizing that we do not collect any personal data. Any information accessed by the app is used solely to display real-time WiFi information within the app window.

1. Information We Access

Location Data

WiFi Ghost may request access to your device's location services to display the WiFi network name (SSID). Due to macOS privacy policies, accessing the SSID requires location services to be enabled. This location data is used exclusively within the app to retrieve and display the SSID in real-time. We do not collect, store, or transmit your location data off your device.

WiFi Network Information

WiFi Ghost accesses the following WiFi network information available on your device:

  • Signal Strength Percentage
  • WiFi Band (e.g., 2.4 GHz or 5 GHz)
  • Channel Number

This information is used solely to display real-time WiFi network details within the app window. We do not collect, store, or transmit this information off your device.

2. How We Use Information

All information accessed by WiFi Ghost is used exclusively to provide real-time WiFi network details within the app window. The app processes this information locally on your device, ensuring that your data remains private and secure.

3. Data Collection, Sharing, and Disclosure

WiFi Ghost does not collect any personal or non-personal data from your device. We do not store any data, nor do we transmit any data off your device. All information accessed by the app is used solely for the purpose of displaying real-time WiFi information within the app window. We do not share any information with third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Since WiFi Ghost does not collect or store any data, there is no data to secure or protect from unauthorized access. All processing occurs locally on your device, and the app relies on macOS's built-in security features to operate.

5. Third-Party Services

WiFi Ghost does not use or integrate with any third-party services. The app operates independently without any external data connections.

6. Children's Privacy

WiFi Ghost does not collect any personal information from users of any age, including children under the age of 13. The app is designed to function without collecting or storing any user data, ensuring privacy for all users.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
